The Best Anime Series on Netflix

There's no denying that in the last decade anime has grown expeditiously. One probable reason: Netflix. The streaming service has heavily invested in the anime universe the catch the attention of the millions of anime fans. Before we knew it, Netflix became the new watch space for the OG watchers and the new comers. Currently, Netflix has a wide variety of genres that are absolutely binge worthy content. This is my list of what I believe SOME of the best anime to watch on Netflix right now. Enjoy!

  • Vinland Saga

    After this father is killed, Thorfinn joins the mercenary band of his murderer Askeladd, waiting for his revenge while Askeladd plots politics.

  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

    After a demon attack leaves his family slain and his sister cursed, Tanjiro embarks upon a journey to find a cure and avenge those he's lost.

  • High-Rise Invasion

    Archangel destroys everything in the name of justice. Mayuko and others stand in his way, but who will die, and who will prevail?

    An herbivore's murder unsettles Cherryton Academy, with some suspecting grey wolf Legoshi.

  • Castlevania

    A headstrong young woman seeks out forbidden knowledge, and a corrupt bishop courts disaster by enraging a legend.

  • Naruto

    Guided by the spirit demon within him, orphaned Naruto learns to harness his powers as a ninja in this anime adventure series.

  • Death Note

    When a high schooler comes into possession of a mystical notebook, he finds he has powers to kill anybody whose name he writes in it.

  • One Punch Man

    The most powerful superhero in the world can kill anyone with one blow, but nothing challenges him, so struggles with ennui and depression.

  • The Seven Deadly Sins

    When a kingdom is taken by tyrants, the princess begins a quest to find a disbanded group of evil knights to help take her back to her realm.

  • The Disastrous Life of Saiki K.
